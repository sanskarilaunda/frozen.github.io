    Ice Pack Pouch


    Gel packs are often made of non-toxic materials that will remain a slow-flowing gel, and therefore will not spill easily or cause contamination even if the container breaks.
    Gel packs are best replacement for wet ice for transportation and temporary storage as they are economic, 2-3 times stay longer and colder than ice. Even it can be reused if not torn or punctured
